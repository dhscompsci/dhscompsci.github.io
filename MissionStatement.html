Dreher High School's Computer Science Club is dedicated to exploring the ever-growing field of computer science. We engage in discussions about the current projects and problems facing today's computer scientists; attend conferences and listen to keynote speakers in the field; explore various programming languages; and build an annual club program/app. The club is also dedicated to promoting the field and emphasizing its importance in each person's daily life. We encourage students with an interest in computers, programming, and innovaiton to consider joining the DHS Computer Science Club.
